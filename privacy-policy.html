Privacy Policy for Storylet

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

Introduction

Storylet ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

Storylet collects minimal information to provide its services:

  • User-Created Content: Stories, text, and images you create are stored locally on your device.
  • User Preferences: Your chosen username, AI assistant name, and voice preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • API Usage: When you use AI features (story generation, image creation), your content is sent to our servers and third-party AI services (Anthropic Claude, OpenAI DALL-E) to generate responses. This data is not stored permanently.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate AI-powered story continuations and illustrations
  • Provide text-to-speech narration
  • Enable PDF and audio export features
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All your stories and preferences are stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage.
  • No Account Required: Storylet does not require account creation or personal identification.
  • Third-Party Services: AI-generated content passes through secure servers but is not retained after processing.

Children's Privacy

Storylet is designed for children and families. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. All data remains on the user's device, and we do not create user profiles or track individual users.

Third-Party Services

Storylet uses the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic Claude: For AI story generation
  • OpenAI DALL-E: For AI image generation

These services process content temporarily to generate responses. Please review their privacy policies:

  • Anthropic: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
  • OpenAI: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except as required to provide the AI features of the app (story and image generation).

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all your stories and data by deleting the app
  • Control what content you create and share
  • Use the app without providing personal information

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I delete a story?
A: On the home screen, tap the trash icon on any story card. You'll be asked to confirm twice to prevent accidental deletion.

Q: Can I edit my stories?
A: Yes! Open any story and tap the Edit button (pencil icon) in the top right. You can edit text, delete lines, remove images, and even change the title.

Q: How do I export my stories?
A: Finish a story by tapping the checkered flag icon. Once finished, you'll see PDF and Audio export options on the home screen for that story.

Q: Why isn't the AI responding?
A: Make sure you have an internet connection. The AI needs to connect to our servers to generate stories and images. If problems persist, try again in a minute.

Q: Is my data safe?
A: Yes! All your stories are stored privately on your device. We don't collect or store your personal information.

We typically respond within 2-3 business days. Thank you for using Storylet!
